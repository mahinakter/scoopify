Frequently Asked Questions 💬

Where do you ship to? +
We ship exclusively within the United States 🇺🇸. We are unable to ship to military addresses, commercial addresses, or P.O. boxes. Please enter a valid residential US address when placing your order.
How do I get free shipping? +
Orders totaling $300 or more qualify for FREE standard shipping! For orders under $300, shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your delivery location within the USA.
When will my order be shipped? +
Current order processing time is 7–10 business days. Once handed over to the carrier, tracking will appear in your order details within a few additional days. Processing time is separate from shipping transit time — thank you for your patience! 💌
What's inside a Mystery Scoop? +
Each scoop is a mystery collection of kawaii goodies — think stationery, accessories, plush charms, keychains, and more! The higher the scoop tier, the more exclusive and valuable the items inside. Every scoop is a surprise! 🎁
Can I return or exchange my scoop? +
Because scoops are mystery items, we do not accept returns or exchanges on opened orders. If your package arrives damaged, please contact us within 48 hours with photos and we'll make it right! 🌸
